Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download movies? +

Click on any movie, then click the "Download" button. This will open our Telegram bot where you can get the file directly.

Is Metflic free to use? +

Yes! Metflic is completely free. We believe entertainment should be accessible to everyone without any subscription fees.

Do I need a Telegram account? +

Yes, you need a Telegram account to download content. Telegram is free and available on all platforms.

How often is new content added? +

New content is added daily. Our team works around the clock to bring you the latest movies and shows.

Can I request specific content? +

Absolutely! Use the contact form above to request specific movies or shows. We do our best to fulfill all requests.

Is the content available in HD? +

Yes, most of our content is available in HD quality (720p or 1080p). Some older titles may be in standard definition.
